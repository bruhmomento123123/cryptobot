Moving Average: (Sum of Prices for n periods) / n
Relative Strength Index (RSI): 100 - [100 / (1 + (Average Gain / Average Loss))]
Bollinger Bands: Middle Band = n-period moving average
Upper Band = Middle Band + (k * n-period standard deviation)
Lower Band = Middle Band - (k * n-period standard deviation)
